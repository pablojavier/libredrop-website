## How It Works

<div class="how-it-works">
  <div class="step">
    <div class="step-number">1</div>
    <h3>Install LibreDrop</h3>
    <p>Download and install on both devices you want to connect.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="step">
    <div class="step-number">2</div>
    <h3>Connect to Wi-Fi</h3>

  <div class="step">
